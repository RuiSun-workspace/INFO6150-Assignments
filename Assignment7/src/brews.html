Brew Methods

Pour-over

Clean, bright cup with nuanced acidity.

  • 15g : 250g
  • Medium-fine
  • ~3:00

French Press

Full-bodied, rich mouthfeel.

  • 18g : 300g
  • Coarse
  • 4:00 steep

Aeropress

Versatile with many recipes.

  • 16g : 220g
  • Medium
  • ~2:00

Espresso

Concentrated and syrupy.

  • 18g in : 36g out
  • Very fine
  • 28–32s

Quick Tips

  • Use filtered water
  • Dial grind size
  • Pre-wet filters
  • Weigh coffee & water
